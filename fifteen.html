Christmas in 15: Puzzle Game

The goal of this puzzle is to un-jingle these cookies so you dont come to dinner empty handed! Your seat at Christmas dinner depends on it! Organize the 15 squares (make it to the grocery store before they close for the next 24 hours) by making the right moves within the board and arrange the 14 movable pieces so the empty space is in the 15th slot. How quickly can you solve it and make it the grocery store?
